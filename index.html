As part of its larger mandate to develop human potential to its fullest, the IIT Delhi has launched an Initiative For Gender Equity and Sensitisation

IGES
Initiative for Gender Equity and Sensitisation was launched on March 8, 2018, with the support of Prof. Ramgopal Rao, Director, IIT Delhi.
IGES, aims to create a safe and violence-free educational atmosphere for all, irrespective of diversities in identities of gender, sex, caste, class, ethnicity, language, race, disability and sexual orientation.
IGES also advocates a zero-tolerance policy against sexual harassment.

Organised 33 workshops on Gender Sensitisation and Sexual Harassment with faculty, staff, security and students.

IGES is developing Code of Conduct for faculty members, students and staff members, on sexual harassment at the workplace.
