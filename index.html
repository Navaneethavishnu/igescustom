IGES
Initiative for Gender Equity and Sensitisation was launched on March 8, 2018, with the support of Prof. Ramgopal Rao, Director, IIT Delhi. IGES, aims to create a safe and violence-free educational atmosphere for all, irrespective of diversities in identities of gender, sex, caste, class, ethnicity, language, race, disability and sexual orientation.IGES also advocates a zero-tolerance policy against sexual harassment.
IGES Mandate
  • Encouraging Gender Dialogues in the field of STEM
  • Advocating a zero-tolerance policy against sexual harassment
  • Creating an equal, inclusive and gender-just educational environment
